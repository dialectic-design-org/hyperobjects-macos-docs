# Hyperobjects

The toolkit derives its name from Hyperobjects as defined by Timothy Morton. Very roughly summarised, the term Hyperobjects points to the direction of entities which exceed the capacities of ordinary human perception.

The book: [Hyperobjects, Philosophy and Ecology after the End of the World](https://www.upress.umn.edu/9780816689231/).
